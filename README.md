## 🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes sales, inventory, and product performance for a chain of toy stores. Using SQL, Excel, and Power BI, the goal is to uncover actionable insights for:  
- Improving sales  
- Optimizing inventory  
- Enhancing profitability  
- Driving data-based strategic decisions

---

## 📊 Dataset
Dataset sourced from [Kaggle: Toys Store Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mysarahmadbhat/toy-sales). Four tables are used:

1. **Products Table** – 35 unique products  
   - `Product_ID`, `Product_Name`, `Product_Category`, `Product_Cost`, `Product_Price`  

2. **Stores Table** – 50 stores  
   - `Store_ID`, `Store_Name`, `Store_City`, `Store_Location`, `Store_Open_Date`  

3. **Sales Table**  
   - `Sale_ID`, `Date`, `Store_ID`, `Product_ID`, `Units`  

4. **Inventory Table**  
   - `Store_ID`, `Product_ID`, `Stock_On_Hand`  

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Store-wise sales and profit analysis  
- Track sales trends over months and seasons  
- Identify best-selling and underperforming products  
- Correlate inventory levels with sales  
- Calculate revenue and profit from remaining stock  
- Provide actionable recommendations for inventory, promotions, and product assortment  

---

## 📈 Analysis Goals
1. **Store-wise Analysis:** Performance, top products, unsold units  
2. **Sales Analysis:** Trends, monthly running totals, high-performing cities  
3. **Inventory Analysis:** Stock vs. sales, product availability, monthly sales  
4. **Product Analysis:** Category performance, top/worst products, daily/monthly sales  
5. **Revenue & Profit Analysis:** Revenue from remaining stock, overall profitability
